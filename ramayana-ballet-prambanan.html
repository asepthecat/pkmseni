The Performance

A Living Epic on Stage

The Ramayana Ballet at Prambanan is one of the most captivating cultural performances in Indonesia. Blending dance and drama without spoken dialogue, it brings the Ramayana story to life through Javanese artistry and tradition.

Since 1961, more than 200 dancers and musicians have filled the open-air stage, with the majestic Prambanan Temple soaring in the background. Every movement, costume, and note of the gamelan creates a rich visual and musical tapestry.

The glow of the stage lights against the stone temples, the ebb and flow of the gamelan, and the expressive storytelling of the dancers draw viewers into Rama's journey to rescue Sita from Ravana.
